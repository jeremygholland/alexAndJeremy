Our Story

  • On a fateful night, Joshua invited his sister out to a New Years eve party. She met Jeremy and the attraction was immediate.

    New Years, 2013

  • Some time passed, as Jeremy lacked the courage to ask the pretty young girl out on a date. He didn't lack the tact, of course, to badger Josh about his "cute sister" every time the two's paths crossed.

    Eventually, Josh did what Jeremy couldn't and set up a date between Jeremy and Alexadra.

    May, 15th 2013

  • From that point, they were inseperable. They already lived close to each other, and spent the majority of the next year together.

    Summer 2013

  • After a year had passed, it was clear that this was going to be a relationship that would stick around. As such, it was time to expand our loving duo into a trio.

    We took to Austin's animal shelter and came home with 19 pounds of What-have-you Terrier. We named him Killer.

    April, 2014

  • We celebrate our first anniversary as a family.

    May 15th, 2014

  • In late 2014, Jeremy and Alex moved into their first apartment together. While the present was glorious and full of love, they began to think towards the future.

    With mounting student loan bills, as well as a need to break the stagnation of a job where Jeremy felt trapped, he began to search for a new future. This lead him to begin to learn web development. This process would involve countless hours of study and work.

    Alex was incredibly supportive of Jeremy in this time, picking him up when he was discouraged and placating him when he was excited.

    Halloween 2014

  • By Christmas time in 2014, our families started to fuse. Jeremy was being integrated into the Keppel clan...

    ... and Alex was becoming a Holland.

    Christmas 2014

  • Over the next year, they grew closer together, and realized that this love was forever.

    May 15th, 2015

  • They celebrated other friends' weddings.

    December, 2015

  • They cooked.

    May, 2016

  • They traveled.

    June, 2016

  • And they decided it was time for them to go ahead and get hitched. In front of the Treaty Oak in downtown Austin, Jeremy proposed to Alexandra.

    She said yes!!!

    September 23th, 2016

  • And.. uh.. That brings us up to today!

    Today
